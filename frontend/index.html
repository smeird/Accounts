What You Can Do

  • Upload OFX files to import your bank statements.
  • Explore your finances through dashboards:
    • Yearly dashboard summarises activity for a selected year.
    • All years dashboard compares trends across years.
    • Monthly dashboard shows month-by-month results.
    • Group dashboard analyses spending by category group.
    • Account dashboard tracks balances per account.
  • Visualise trends with interactive graphs.
  • Track spending against budgets.
  • View monthly statements to review individual transactions.
  • Generate detailed transaction reports.
  • Search transactions to find specific payments or receipts.
  • Track transfers between accounts separately.
  • Organise data by managing tags, categories and groups.
  • Identify untagged items via the Missing Tags page.
  • Run background processes and review application logs.
  • Back up and restore your data.
  • Manage user accounts and log out when you're done.
